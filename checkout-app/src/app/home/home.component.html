Get notified when home delivery slots become available at supermarkets

COVID-19 has made it difficult to shop for groceries and online delivery slots are hard to find.

Our service automatically monitors the availabilty of home delivery slots at many of the UK's leading supermarkets and notifies you once a slot becomes available.

This is completely free for all key workers and vulnerable people to support them in these challenging times.

How it works

Log in and select the supermarket you want a slot for.

Once you’ve made your selection, we’ll search for available delivery slots.

When we find an available delivery slot, we will automatically notify you.

Are you a key worker?

Key workers are people whose jobs are critical to the health of the nation during the COVID-19 pandemic and public lockdown. The UK Government is keen to ensure that they can continue to carry out their jobs with as little restriction as possible. These include:

  • Health and social care workers
  • Education and childcare staff
  • Utility workers
  • Public transport staff
